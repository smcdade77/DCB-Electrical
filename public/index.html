Electrical Estimating — FAQs

These target long‑tail keywords contractors search for.

What’s included in your electrical estimating service?

Quantities (takeoffs), labor units, material lists, allowance assumptions, alternates/VE, clarifications, and a priced proposal that’s easy to level.

Which software do you use for takeoffs and pricing?

Accubid for labor/material databases and pricing structure; on‑screen takeoff tools (LiveCount or your preferred tool) for on‑screen takeoffs. We can also work in your templates if preferred.

Do you support federal (USACE/NAVFAC) bids?

Yes—our deliverables reflect spec formatting, BAA/TAA sourcing constraints, and common Section 26 requirements.

How fast can you turn around a bid?

Typical small/medium bids: 3–7 business days depending on complexity. Rush options available when feasible.

Can you stay on after award?

Yes. We provide buyout, planning, PM support, change pricing, and closeout—so estimates connect cleanly to delivery.

How do fees work?

Fixed‑fee per bid, hourly support, or capacity blocks for ongoing programs. Transparent scope and updates each week.
